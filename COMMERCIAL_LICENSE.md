# Commercial Licensing — HomeEdge Orchestrator

HomeEdge Orchestrator is available under the **Business Source License (BSL 1.1)** for community use and evaluation.

Commercial use beyond the scope of the BSL requires a commercial license.

This document explains when a commercial license is required and how to obtain one.

---

## When you need a commercial license

A commercial license is required if you intend to use HomeEdge in a way that directly generates revenue or is part of a commercial product or service.

This includes (but is not limited to):

### Restricted uses

You must obtain a commercial license if you intend to:

- Offer HomeEdge as a SaaS or hosted service
- Provide HomeEdge as part of a managed service
- Resell HomeEdge or derivatives
- Embed HomeEdge into a commercial product
- Provide HomeEdge as part of a paid platform
- Use HomeEdge to operate infrastructure for paying customers
- Distribute modified commercial versions

### Uses that typically do NOT require a commercial license

These uses are allowed under the BSL:

- Personal projects
- Educational use
- Research
- Evaluation
- Internal infrastructure use within your own company
- Non-profit projects
- Contributions to the project

If unsure, contact us.

## Commercial licensing philosophy

HomeEdge follows a **fair commercial open-source model**:

The source code is open for learning, contribution, and internal use, while commercial resale or platform usage requires licensing.

This model allows:

- open collaboration
- sustainable development
- continued maintenance
- long-term project evolution
